Faculty Overview

The Faculty of Art, Design & Architecture at the University of Johannesburg offers an array of programs and disciplines that explore the creative arts, architecture, design, and media. Our students are empowered to innovate and transform society through design thinking and creative problem-solving.

Funding and Scholarships

We provide various funding opportunities for students, including scholarships and bursaries for both local and international students. Scholarships are awarded based on merit, need, and specific faculty requirements. Check out the available scholarships:

  • Art & Design Merit Scholarship: Awarded to top-performing students in the arts and design programs.
  • Architecture Future Leaders Fund: A scholarship for architecture students focused on innovative design solutions.
  • Global Creativity Grant: Available to international students pursuing degrees in art or architecture.

Public Research

Our faculty is committed to pushing the boundaries of knowledge and research in art, design, and architecture. Recent research projects include:

  • The Future of Sustainable Design: A research project focused on integrating sustainability into modern architecture.
  • Digital Arts Innovation: Exploring the intersection of technology and traditional art forms.
  • Urbanization and Space: A study on how architecture can improve urban living conditions in rapidly growing cities.

Recent Events

Our faculty frequently hosts events, exhibitions, and workshops for students and the community. Here are some of our recent events:

  • Art & Design Showcase 2025: A biennial event where students display their creative works to the public.
  • Urban Architecture Forum: A recent forum with industry leaders discussing the future of urban design.
  • Creative Digital Arts Symposium: A virtual event focused on the convergence of art and technology.
